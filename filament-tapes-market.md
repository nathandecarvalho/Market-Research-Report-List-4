<p><p>Filament tapes market consists of two main types: single sided tapes and double sided tapes. Single sided tapes have adhesive on one side and are commonly used for applications where high tensile strength is required. Double sided tapes have adhesive on both sides and are often used for bonding two surfaces together. Both types of tapes are popular in industries such as packaging, construction, and automotive for their strong adhesive properties and durability.</p></p>
