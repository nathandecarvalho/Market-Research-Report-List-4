<p><p>Limb salvage systems are medical devices used in orthopedic surgeries to help preserve a limb that is at risk of amputation. There are different types of limb salvage systems available, including allo-metal prostheses, metal prostheses, and allografts. Allo-metal prostheses combine biological and mechanical components, metal prostheses are made entirely of metal, and allografts involve using tissue from a donor to replace damaged bone. Each type of limb salvage system has its own advantages and can be tailored to meet the specific needs of a patient.</p></p>
