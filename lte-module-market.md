<p><strong>The LTE Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPC (Evolved Packet Core)</li><li>ENode B (Evolved Node B)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LTE Module market can be segmented into two types: EPC (Evolved Packet Core) and ENode B (Evolved Node B). EPC is the central core network of LTE that manages the packet data between the LTE module and the external networks. ENode B is the LTE base station that connects the LTE module to the core network. Both EPC and ENode B play crucial roles in ensuring seamless communication and data transfer within LTE networks, making them essential components in the LTE module market.</p></p>
