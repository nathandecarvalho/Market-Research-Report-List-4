<p><p>Cassava powder is available in two main types - sweet cassava powder and bitter cassava powder. Sweet cassava powder is made from the roots of the sweet cassava plant, which has a mild taste and is commonly used for baking and cooking. On the other hand, bitter cassava powder is made from the roots of the bitter cassava plant, which contains toxins that must be properly processed before consumption. Bitter cassava powder is mainly used for industrial purposes such as in pharmaceuticals and biofuels.</p></p>
