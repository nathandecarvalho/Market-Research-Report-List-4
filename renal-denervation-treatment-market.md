There are two main types of renal denervation treatment - Radiofrequency and Ultrasound. Radiofrequency involves using thermal energy to deactivate the nerves, while Ultrasound uses sound waves to achieve the same effect. Both methods have shown promising results in lowering blood pressure for patients with resistant hypertension. The market for these treatments is growing as more individuals seek alternative solutions for managing their blood pressure effectively.</p></p>
