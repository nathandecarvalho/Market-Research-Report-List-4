<p><strong>The Decorative Laminates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-pressure Laminate</li><li>Low-pressure Laminate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Decorative laminates market can be divided into two main types: high-pressure laminate (HPL) and low-pressure laminate (LPL). HPL is a durable and impact-resistant option often used for countertops and furniture surfaces. It is created by bonding multiple layers of Kraft paper with a resin under high heat and pressure. On the other hand, LPL is a more cost-effective option used for interior applications such as cabinets and furniture. It is made by applying a decorative paper to a substrate with heat and pressure.</p></p>
