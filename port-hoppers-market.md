<p><ul><li>Static Hopper</li><li>Rail Hopper</li><li>Wheeled Hopper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Port Hoppers are a critical component of port operations, used for transferring bulk materials such as coal, grains, and minerals. There are three main types of Port Hoppers: Static Hoppers, Rail Hoppers, and Wheeled Hoppers. Static Hoppers are fixed in location and are used for unloading materials from ships directly onto trucks or conveyor belts. Rail Hoppers are mounted on railway tracks for easy movement of materials within the port. Wheeled Hoppers are mobile units that can be easily transported around the port to access different areas for loading and unloading materials.</p></p>
