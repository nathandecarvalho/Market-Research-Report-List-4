<p><p>Crystalline Silicon PV market consists of two main types of modules: Mono-Si and Multi-Si. Mono-Si modules are made from high-quality silicon crystals with a single crystal structure, offering higher efficiency and reliability but at a higher cost. Multi-Si modules, on the other hand, are made from silicon crystals with multiple crystal structures, providing a more cost-effective option with slightly lower efficiency. Both types of modules are commonly used in the solar industry, catering to different market segments based on cost and performance requirements.</p></p>
