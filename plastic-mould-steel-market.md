<p><p>Plastic mould steel market consists of various types such as carburizing type, pre-hardened, age hardening type, and corrosion-resistant type. Carburizing type steel is treated to increase surface hardness. Pre-hardened steel does not require heat treatment after machining. Age hardening steel achieves high strength through aging at a specific temperature. Corrosion-resistant steel is made to withstand rust and corrosion. These different types cater to the diverse needs of industries using plastic moulds, offering options for different hardness levels and resistance properties.</p></p>
