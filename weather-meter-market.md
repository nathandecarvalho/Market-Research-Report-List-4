<p><strong>The Weather Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ground Weather Instrument</li><li>Upper-air Weather Instrument</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ground weather instruments are tools used to measure weather parameters close to the Earth's surface, such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation. These instruments are crucial for forecasting daily weather conditions and monitoring local climate patterns. In contrast, upper-air weather instruments are designed to collect data from the atmosphere at higher altitudes, providing information on air pressure, temperature, humidity, and wind patterns. Together, ground and upper-air weather instruments help meteorologists analyze weather patterns and make accurate forecasts.</p></p>
