<p><p>Wearable pulse oximeters come in various types, including fingertip, ring, and wrist-worn devices. Fingertip pulse oximeters are small, portable devices that attach to the fingertip to measure oxygen levels in the blood. Ring pulse oximeters function similarly but are worn on the finger like a ring. Wrist-worn pulse oximeters are worn like a watch and provide continuous monitoring of oxygen saturation levels. Each type offers convenience and real-time data for users monitoring their oxygen levels on the go.</p></p>
