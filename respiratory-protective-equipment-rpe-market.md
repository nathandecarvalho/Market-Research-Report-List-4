<p><p>Respiratory Protective Equipment (RPE) includes Non-Powered Air Respirators (NPAR), Powered Air Respirators (PAR), and Self Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA). NPAR relies on the wearer's breathing to filter out contaminants, while PAR uses a powered fan to assist with breathing. SCBA provides a portable air supply for users in oxygen-deficient or hazardous environments. Each type offers varying levels of protection and user comfort, catering to different industry needs such as construction, healthcare, mining, and firefighting.</p></p>
