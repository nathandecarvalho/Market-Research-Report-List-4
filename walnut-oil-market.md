<p><strong>The Walnut Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold-Pressed Walnut Oil</li><li>Expeller-Pressed Walnut Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Walnut oil comes in two main types: cold-pressed and expeller-pressed. Cold-pressed walnut oil is made by gently pressing walnuts to extract the oil without using heat, which helps preserve its natural flavors and nutrients. This type of oil is popular in gourmet cooking and as a finishing oil. On the other hand, expeller-pressed walnut oil is made by using mechanical pressure to extract the oil, making it more suitable for high-heat cooking methods. Both types offer distinct flavors and uses in the culinary world.</p></p>
