<p><p>Membrane pumps market comprises two main types: Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps and Electric Driven Diaphragm Pumps. Air Operated Diaphragm Pumps are powered by compressed air, making them suitable for applications where electricity is not readily available or poses a safety hazard. Electric Driven Diaphragm Pumps, on the other hand, are powered by electricity and offer a more consistent and controlled flow rate. Both types of pumps are commonly used in industries such as wastewater treatment, chemical processing, and food and beverage production.</p></p>
