<p><strong>The Power-over-Ethernet (PoE) Chipsets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PoE Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Chipset</li><li>PoE Powered Devices (PD) Chipset</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power-over-Ethernet (PoE) chipsets are essential components in PoE technology, enabling the transmission of power and data over a single Ethernet cable. The PoE Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) chipsets are used in power sourcing devices such as switches and injectors, providing power to PoE Powered Devices (PD) such as IP cameras, access points, and VoIP phones. PSE chipsets manage power delivery, while PD chipsets receive and regulate power, allowing for the seamless operation of PoE-enabled devices in various applications.</p></p>
