<p><p>Wireless Intrusion Detection Systems (WIDS) are passive security measures that monitor wireless networks for unauthorized access or malicious activities. They detect and alert administrators of potential threats. On the other hand, Wireless Intrusion Prevention Systems (WIPS) actively block and prevent unauthorized access or attacks on wireless networks. Both WIDS and WIPS play a crucial role in securing wireless networks and are essential components of an organization's overall cybersecurity strategy.</p></p>
