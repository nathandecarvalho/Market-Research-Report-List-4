<p><p>Hospital lighting market includes various types of lighting fixtures such as troffers, surface-mounted lights, and surgical lamps. Troffers are recessed lighting fixtures mounted on the ceiling in a grid pattern, providing even illumination. Surface-mounted lights are installed directly on the ceiling, offering flexibility in positioning and easy installation. Surgical lamps are specialized lights used in operating rooms, providing focused and adjustable lighting for surgical procedures. Each type of lighting fixture serves a specific purpose in providing efficient and effective lighting in hospital settings.</p></p>
