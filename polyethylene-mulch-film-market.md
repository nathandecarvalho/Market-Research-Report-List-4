<p><strong>The Polyethylene Mulch Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LLDPE</li><li>LDPE</li><li>HDPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyethylene mulch film is available in three types: LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene). LLDPE is known for its flexibility and puncture resistance, making it ideal for uneven surfaces. LDPE offers excellent tear resistance, while HDPE provides superior durability and stiffness. Each type caters to different agricultural needs, with LLDPE suitable for fruits and vegetables, LDPE for row crops, and HDPE for heavy-duty applications such as landfill cover or pond lining.</p></p>
