<p><ul><li>Manual-hydraulic Version</li><li>Electro-mechanical Version</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Two-stage lifting columns are commonly found in manual-hydraulic and electro-mechanical versions. The manual-hydraulic version is operated using a pump handle to manually raise and lower the loads. On the other hand, the electro-mechanical version uses an electric motor to provide power for raising and lowering the loads with the push of a button. Both types are widely used in industries such as automotive, manufacturing, and warehouses for tasks that require precise and controlled lifting operations.</p></p>
