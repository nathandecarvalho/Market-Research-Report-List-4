<p><p>Aluminum alloy automotive sheet market is divided into three main types: Cast Aluminum, Rolled Aluminum, and Extruded Aluminum. Cast aluminum sheets are formed through a casting process, resulting in high strength and density. Rolled aluminum sheets are processed through rolling mills, making them lightweight and flexible. Extruded aluminum sheets are created by forcing the metal through a die, producing complex shapes and designs.
