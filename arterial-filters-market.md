<p><p>Arterial filters are medical devices used during cardiothoracic surgeries to remove embolic materials from blood flow. There are two main types of arterial filters available in the market: adult arterial filters and pediatric arterial filters designed for children. Adult arterial filters are larger in size to accommodate the higher blood flow rate in adults, while pediatric arterial filters are smaller and more finely tuned to meet the specific needs of children undergoing heart surgeries. Both types of filters play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and success of cardiac procedures.</p></p>
