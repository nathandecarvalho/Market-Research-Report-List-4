<p><p>The baby car seat market consists of three main types: forward-facing car seats, booster seats, and rear-facing car seats. Forward-facing car seats are designed for children who have outgrown rear-facing seats and provide protection in the event of a crash by securing the child with a harness. Booster seats elevate children to the appropriate height for seat belts to fit properly. Rear-facing car seats are recommended for infants and young children, offering crucial support for their developing bodies in the event of a collision.</p></p>
