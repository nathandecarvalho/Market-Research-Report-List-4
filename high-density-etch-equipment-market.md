Wet Etching Equipment involves using liquid chemicals to selectively remove materials, suitable for larger scale production. Both types of equipment cater to different needs in the semiconductor industry, with dry etching equipment being favored for its accuracy and wet etching equipment for its cost-effectiveness and simplicity.</p></p>
