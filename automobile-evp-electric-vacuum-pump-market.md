<p><p>The Automobile EVP (Electric Vacuum Pump) market consists of three main types: diaphragm type, leaf type, and swing pistol type. Diaphragm type EVPs use a flexible diaphragm to create vacuum pressure, while leaf type EVPs use spring-loaded metal leaves. Swing pistol type EVPs have a swinging piston mechanism to generate vacuum. Each type has its own unique design and functionality, catering to different applications and requirements within the automotive industry.</p></p>
