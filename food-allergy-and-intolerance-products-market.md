<p><p>Food allergy and intolerance products market includes two main types: Immunoglobulin E (IgE) Mediated Food Allergy and Non-IgE Mediated Food Allergy. IgE Mediated Food Allergy is an immediate immune system reaction to specific food proteins, leading to symptoms such as hives, swelling, and anaphylaxis. Non-IgE Mediated Food Allergy involves a delayed immune response, causing symptoms like eczema, vomiting, and diarrhea. Both types of food allergies require specialized products, such as allergen-free foods and dietary supplements, to manage symptoms and prevent reactions.</p></p>
