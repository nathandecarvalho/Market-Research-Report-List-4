<p><p>Squeeze casting machines are categorized into two main types, hot chamber and cold chamber machines. Hot chamber squeeze casting machines are designed for casting materials with low melting points such as zinc, magnesium, and lead. These machines have a built-in melting furnace, allowing for easier material handling and faster production. On the other hand, cold chamber squeeze casting machines are used for casting materials with higher melting points such as aluminum and copper. These machines require a separate melting furnace for material preparation before casting.</p></p>
