<p><p>A covered stent is a medical device used in the treatment of various cardiovascular conditions, to provide a barrier between the blood flow and the vessel wall, preventing restenosis or blockages. These stents are usually made from a combination of metal and a fabric material, which helps in maintaining blood flow within the vessel and reducing the risk of complications.</p><p>The covered stent market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period, with a projected CAGR of 7.9%. The increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, along with the rising demand for minimally invasive procedures, is driving the growth of the market. Additionally, advancements in technology and innovations in stent design are also contributing to the market expansion.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the covered stent market include the development of bioresorbable stents, which are designed to dissolve over time, reducing the risk of long-term complications. Furthermore, the rising adoption of drug-eluting covered stents, which release medication to prevent restenosis, is expected to further boost market growth. Overall, the covered stent market is poised for significant growth in the coming years, driven by technological advancements and increasing demand for minimally invasive cardiovascular procedures.</p></p>

<p><p>Covered stent market includes AAA Stent Grafts and TAA Stent Grafts. AAA Stent Grafts are used in the treatment of abdominal aortic aneurysms, a condition where the lower part of the aorta balloons out. TAA Stent Grafts are used in the treatment of thoracic aortic aneurysms, which occur in the upper part of the aorta. Both these stent grafts involve the placement of a covered stent to reinforce the weakened blood vessel and prevent further expansion or rupture of the aneurysm.</p></p>
