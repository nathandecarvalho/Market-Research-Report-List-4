<p><p>The plastic ventilation fan market can be categorized into two main types: Axial and Centrifugal fans. Axial fans are designed with blades that rotate around an axis to create air flow, making them ideal for applications requiring high air volume at low pressure. On the other hand, Centrifugal fans use a rotating impeller to increase the air pressure, making them suitable for applications that require higher pressure levels. Both types of fans are widely used in various industries for ventilation and air circulation purposes.</p></p>
