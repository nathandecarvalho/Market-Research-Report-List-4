<p><p>Vacuum gate valves are classified into two main types - pneumatic and manual. Pneumatic gate valves are operated using compressed air to open and close the valve, providing quick and efficient control. On the other hand, manual gate valves require manual operation by turning a handwheel or lever to control the flow of the valve. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements, with pneumatic valves offering automation and precision, while manual valves provide simplicity and reliability.</p></p>
