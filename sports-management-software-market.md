<p><p>Sports management software can be categorized into two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-based. On-Premise software is installed directly onto a company's local servers and maintained by their own IT team. This type of software is typically more customizable and offers higher data security but requires significant upfront costs. On the other hand, Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, accessible through the internet, and managed by the software provider. This type is more cost-effective, scalable, and offers easier access to real-time data.</p></p>
