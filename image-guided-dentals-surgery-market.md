<p><p>Image Guided Dental Surgery involves the use of imaging technologies such as X-ray and Computed Tomography (CT) to assist dentists in planning and carrying out surgical procedures with precision. X-ray imaging provides 2D images of the teeth and jaw bone, allowing dentists to identify issues such as cavities or bone loss. CT imaging, on the other hand, produces detailed 3D images that enable dentists to visualize the structure of the jaw bone and surrounding tissues, helping them to plan complex procedures like dental implant placement.</p></p>
