<p><p>High Temperature Fluoropolymers (High Temperature FPs) are known for their exceptional thermal stability, chemical resistance, and low friction properties. High Performance Polyamide (HPPA) offer durability and strength at high temperatures. Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) exhibit excellent mechanical properties and chemical resistance. Sulfone Polymers (SP) provide high impact strength and heat resistance. Liquid Crystal Polymers (LCP) offer dimensional stability and heat resistance. Aromatic Ketone Polymers (AKP) provide high mechanical strength and chemical resistance. Poly-imide (PI) have excellent thermal and chemical resistance, making them suitable for high temperature applications.</p></p>
