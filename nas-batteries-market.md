<p><p>NaS batteries are categorized into two main types: Molten-Salt Type and Others. Molten-Salt Type NaS batteries utilize a liquid sodium electrode, a beta alumina solid electrolyte, and a molten salt cathode, resulting in high energy density and long cycle life. On the other hand, Other NaS batteries use different materials for electrodes and electrolytes, offering variations in performance and cost. Both types cater to different market needs and demand for energy storage solutions in various industries.</p></p>
