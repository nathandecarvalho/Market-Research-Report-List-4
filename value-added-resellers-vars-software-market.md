This model often requires a significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, in the cloud-based market, VARs sell software that is hosted and maintained by a third-party provider, allowing for easy scalability, lower upfront costs, and increased flexibility. This model is gaining popularity as businesses embrace digital transformation and remote work solutions.</p></p>
