Solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents for application, Water-borne coatings use water as a dispersing medium, High Solids coatings have a lower amount of solvent content, and Powder coatings are applied as a dry powder onto the surface. Each type caters to different needs in terms of performance, environmental impact, and application process, providing a wide range of options for various industries.</p></p>
