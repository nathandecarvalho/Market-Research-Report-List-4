<p><p>Multi Factor Authentication System comes in various types depending on the number of factors required for authentication. Two Factor market is the most common, requiring two different factors such as a password and a code sent to a phone. Three Factor market adds a biometric factor like fingerprint recognition. Four Factor market includes a hardware token for added security. Five Factor market is the most advanced, combining something you know, something you have, something you are, somewhere you are, and something you do for comprehensive authentication.</p></p>
