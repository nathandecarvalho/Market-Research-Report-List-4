<p><p>Hydraulic actuators are devices that convert hydraulic energy into mechanical motion. There are two main types of hydraulic actuators: linear actuators and rotary actuators. Linear actuators produce linear motion, moving in a straight line, while rotary actuators produce rotary motion, rotating around an axis. Linear actuators are commonly used in applications such as lifting and pushing, while rotary actuators are used for applications like turning and positioning. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
