<p><p>Centralized Automated Dispensing Systems are typically found in hospitals or large healthcare facilities where medications are stored in a centralized location and dispensed to patients based on orders from healthcare providers. These systems are often integrated with electronic health records for accurate dispensing and tracking of medications. </p><p>Decentralized Automated Dispensing Systems, on the other hand, are more commonly used in long-term care facilities or pharmacies where medications are stored in multiple locations throughout the facility and dispensed to patients by nurses or staff members.
