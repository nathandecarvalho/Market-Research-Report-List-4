<p><p>Ice cream toppings can be categorized into four main types: sequins, needles, beads, and others. Sequins are small, shiny discs that add a touch of sparkle to the ice cream. Needles are thin, stick-shaped toppings that provide a crunchy texture. Beads are small, spherical toppings that come in a variety of colors and flavors. The "others" category includes a wide range of toppings such as sprinkles, nuts, fruits, and candies. Each type of topping offers a unique taste and texture to enhance the enjoyment of ice cream.</p></p>
