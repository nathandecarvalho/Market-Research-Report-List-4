<p><ul><li>Wave Type</li><li>Drum Type</li><li>Stirring Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Washing machine clutches come in three main types: Wave Type, Drum Type, and Stirring Type. Wave Type clutches use a wave spring to engage the drum and agitate the clothes during the wash cycle. Drum Type clutches operate by connecting the drum directly to the motor, providing a more efficient and powerful wash. Stirring Type clutches use paddle-like agitators to move clothes around in the drum, ensuring a thorough and even clean. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the specific needs of the consumer.</p></p>
