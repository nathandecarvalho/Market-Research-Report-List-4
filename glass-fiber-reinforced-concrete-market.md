<p><p>Glass Fiber Reinforced Concrete market types are categorized based on compressive strength expressed in terms of megapascals. Under C30 denotes products with compressive strength below 30 MPa, suitable for non-structural applications. C30-C60 offers stronger concrete suitable for structural projects. C60-C100 provides even higher strength for heavy-duty structures. Above C100 signifies the strongest concrete mix with compressive strength exceeding 100 MPa, ideal for specialized applications requiring extreme durability. These market types cater to a wide range of construction needs with varying levels of strength and performance.</p></p>
