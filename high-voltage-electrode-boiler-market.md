<p><p>The High Voltage Electrode Boiler Market consists of three main types: Fully Submerged Electrode Boiler, Semi-immersed Electrode Boiler, and Jet Electrode Boiler. Fully Submerged Electrode Boilers are completely immersed in water, providing efficient heating. Semi-immersed Electrode Boilers have part of the electrode immersed in water, allowing for flexibility in operation. Jet Electrode Boilers use high-velocity jets of water to generate steam, offering high efficiency and precise control. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market for high voltage electrode boilers.</p></p>
