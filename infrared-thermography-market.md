<p><p>Infrared thermography market consists of laser and structured light technologies. Laser thermography uses laser sensors to measure temperature variations on a surface, providing accurate and precise results. On the other hand, structured light thermography projects patterns onto a surface and captures the distortion caused by temperature differences, offering detailed thermal imaging. Both technologies are widely used in various industries such as manufacturing, construction, and healthcare for detecting heat loss, monitoring equipment, and conducting inspections.</p></p>
