<p><strong>The Electronic Voltage Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Electronic Voltage Transformer</li><li>Passive Electronic Voltage Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Electronic Voltage Transformers are devices that use active electronic components to step up or step down voltages. They are capable of adjusting the output voltage based on the input voltage, providing greater flexibility and control. On the other hand, Passive Electronic Voltage Transformers rely on passive electronic components such as resistors, capacitors, and inductors to achieve voltage transformation. They are simpler in design and offer a more cost-effective solution, albeit with less customization options compared to active transformers. Both types cater to the diverse needs of the voltage transformation market.</p></p>
