<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>Digital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Two-way radios are commonly available in two main market types: analog and digital. Analog radios use a continuous electromagnetic wave to transmit and receive signals, while digital radios convert voice into binary code for transmission. Analog radios are traditional and cost-effective, but may have lower audio quality and security compared to digital radios. Digital radios offer improved clarity, security, and range, along with additional features like text messaging and GPS tracking. Both types have their own set of advantages and disadvantages, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
