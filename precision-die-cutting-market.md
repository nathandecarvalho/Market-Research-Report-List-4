<p><p>Precision die cutting is a manufacturing process that involves cutting materials into specific shapes or patterns. The three main types of precision die cutting are flat bed die cutting, rotary die cutting, and waterjet cutting. Flat bed die cutting involves using a flat surface to cut materials, while rotary die cutting uses a cylindrical die to cut materials during continuous operation. Waterjet cutting utilizes a high-pressure stream of water to cut materials accurately and efficiently. Each type of precision die cutting has its own set of benefits and applications.</p></p>
