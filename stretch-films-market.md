<p><p>Stretch films are widely used for packaging and protecting goods during transportation and storage. The market is divided into three main types based on the material used: Polyethylene (PE) stretch films are the most common and versatile, offering good tensile strength and puncture resistance. Polypropylene (PP) stretch films are lightweight and offer high clarity and excellent stretch capabilities. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) stretch films have superior tear resistance and are often used for bundling irregularly shaped items. Each type caters to different packaging needs in various industries.</p></p>
