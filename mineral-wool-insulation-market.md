<p><p>Mineral wool insulation is divided into two main types: glass wool and stone wool. Glass wool insulation is made from recycled glass and offers good thermal and acoustic properties. Stone wool insulation, on the other hand, is made from volcanic rock and is highly durable and fire-resistant. Both types of mineral wool insulation are popular choices for residential and commercial buildings due to their energy efficiency and ability to regulate indoor temperatures.</p></p>
