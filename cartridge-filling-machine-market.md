<p><p>Cartridge filling machines come in three main types - fully-automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully-automatic machines require minimal human intervention as they can fill cartridges automatically with high efficiency. Semi-automatic machines require some operator input for loading and unloading cartridges but can still fill them quickly. Manual machines require complete manual operation, making them slower and less efficient compared to automatic options. Each type caters to different production needs and levels of automation in the cartridge filling process.</p></p>
