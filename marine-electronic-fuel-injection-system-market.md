<p><strong>The Marine Electronic Fuel Injection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pump-Line-Nozzle System</li><li>Common Rail System</li><li>Other Injection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine Electronic Fuel Injection System market encompasses various types, including Pump-Line-Nozzle System, Common Rail System, and Other Injection Systems. The Pump-Line-Nozzle System delivers fuel in individual lines to each cylinder through separate fuel pumps. Common Rail System supplies fuel to a common rail that controls the amount injected into each cylinder. Other Injection Systems refer to alternative methods of injecting fuel, such as direct injection or indirect injection. Each type offers unique benefits and caters to different marine engine requirements.</p></p>
