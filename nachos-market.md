<p><p>Nachos are a popular snack enjoyed in various markets, available in two main types: fried and baked. Fried nachos are typically crispy and savory, made by deep-frying corn tortilla chips until golden and crunchy. They are commonly found in fast-food establishments and casual dining restaurants. Baked nachos, on the other hand, are prepared by layering tortilla chips with toppings and cheese, then baking them in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Baked nachos are often considered a healthier option and can be found in health food stores and cafes.</p></p>
