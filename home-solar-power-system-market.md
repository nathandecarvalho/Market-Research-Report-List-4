<p><p>The Home Solar Power System Market includes three main types: Pitched Roof System, Flat Roof System, and Ground Mounted System. Pitched Roof System is installed on sloped roofs of residential buildings, taking advantage of the angle for maximum sunlight exposure. Flat Roof System is suitable for commercial buildings with flat roofs, providing a large surface area for solar panels. Ground Mounted System is installed on the ground, ideal for homes with limited roof space or for larger installations. Each type offers unique benefits depending on the property's requirements.</p></p>
