<p><p>Composite coatings market includes various types such as Laser Melt Injection, Brazing, Electroless Plating, and others. Laser Melt Injection involves injecting a coating material onto a surface using a high-energy laser beam. Brazing involves joining materials by melting a filler metal that flows into the joint. Electroless Plating uses chemical reactions to deposit a thin layer of metal onto a substrate. Other types of composite coatings may include thermal spraying, chemical vapor deposition, and physical vapor deposition methods.</p></p>
