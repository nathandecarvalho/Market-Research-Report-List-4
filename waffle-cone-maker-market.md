<p><p>The Waffle Cone Maker market includes two main types: Heat Both Sides and Heat One Side. Heat Both Sides machines cook batter simultaneously on both sides, resulting in a fully cooked and evenly browned waffle cone. On the other hand, Heat One Side machines only cook batter on one side at a time, requiring the operator to manually flip the cone to ensure both sides are cooked evenly. Each type caters to different preferences and production needs within the market.</p></p>
