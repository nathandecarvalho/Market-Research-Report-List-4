Microwave systems operate by transmitting signals between a handheld remote and a receiver in the vehicle using high-frequency radio waves. Radio frequency systems utilize lower frequencies to communicate between the remote and vehicle receiver. Other markets may include infrared or Bluetooth technology for keyless entry. Each type offers unique features and benefits for users seeking convenient and reliable access to their vehicles.</p></p>
