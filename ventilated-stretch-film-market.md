<p><p>The Ventilated Stretch Film market consists of two main types: Perforated Manual Film and Perforated Machine Film. Perforated Manual Film is designed for manual application and typically comes in rolls that are perforated for easy tearing. This type of film is commonly used for wrapping individual or small quantities of products. On the other hand, Perforated Machine Film is designed for use with stretch wrapping machines and is available in larger rolls with perforations for automated application. Both types offer ventilation properties to allow for airflow while providing secure packaging.</p></p>
