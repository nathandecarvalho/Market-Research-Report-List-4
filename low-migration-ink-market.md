<p><p>Low migration ink is used in various printing processes like gravure, flexography, offset, and digital printing. Gravure printing involves etching the image onto a cylinder, making it ideal for high-quality printing. Flexography uses flexible plates and is commonly used for packaging. Offset printing transfers the image onto a rubber blanket before printing, providing sharp and clear images. Digital printing directly transfers the digital image onto the material. Each market type has its own benefits and applications, with low migration ink ensuring safe printing for food and pharmaceutical packaging.</p></p>
