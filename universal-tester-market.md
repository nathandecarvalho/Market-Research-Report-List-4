<p><ul><li>Electromechanical UTM</li><li>Hydraulic UTM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Universal Tester (UTM) market consists of two main types: Electromechanical UTM and Hydraulic UTM. </p><p>Electromechanical UTM utilizes an electric motor to apply force, making it suitable for testing applications that require high precision and controlled loading rates. On the other hand, Hydraulic UTM uses hydraulic pressure to apply force, making it suitable for high-capacity testing applications requiring maximum force capabilities. Both types offer different advantages and features, catering to a wide range of testing needs in various industries.</p></p>
