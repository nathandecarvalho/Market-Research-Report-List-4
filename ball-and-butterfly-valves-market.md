<p><p>Ball and butterfly valves are types of quarter-turn valves that regulate or control the flow of liquids or gases in a pipeline. Ball valves have a spherical closure element that rotates to open and close the valve, while butterfly valves have a disc-shaped closure element that rotates on a perpendicular axis to control flow. Both types of valves are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, water and wastewater, chemical processing, and power generation.</p><p>The global ball and butterfly valves market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
