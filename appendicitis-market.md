<p><p>Appendicitis can be classified into two main market types: Acute and Chronic. Acute appendicitis refers to the sudden inflammation of the appendix, often requiring immediate surgical intervention. It is a rapidly progressing condition that can be life-threatening if not treated promptly. On the other hand, Chronic appendicitis is a more long-lasting form of the disease with milder symptoms that come and go over time. Both types of appendicitis can cause pain, fever, and gastrointestinal issues, but they differ in severity and duration.</p></p>
