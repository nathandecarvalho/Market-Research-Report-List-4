<p><p>Lithium Triborate (LBO) crystals are widely used in various applications such as Second Harmonic Generation (SHG), Sum Frequency Generation (SFG), and others. In SHG, LBO crystals are utilized to double the frequency of input laser beams, making them valuable in applications like laser cutting and welding. SFG involves combining two input beams to generate a new one with a frequency equal to the sum of the inputs, useful in spectroscopy and chemical analysis. LBO crystals also find application in electro-optical devices and optical parametric oscillators.</p></p>
