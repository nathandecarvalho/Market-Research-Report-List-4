<p><ul><li>Explosion Bonded</li><li>Roll Bonded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Composite plate market consists of two major types: Explosion bonded and Roll bonded. Explosion bonded plates are created by using explosive energy to bond two or more metals together, resulting in a strong and durable composite plate. Roll bonded plates, on the other hand, are created by rolling two or more metal layers together under high pressure, providing a cost-effective and efficient way to produce composite plates. Both types offer unique advantages and are utilized in various industries for their superior strength and performance.</p></p>
