<p><strong>The Lidar Systems for Wind Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ground-mounted Lidar systems</li><li>Nacelle-mounted Lidar systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ground-mounted Lidar systems are commonly used in wind energy applications to measure wind characteristics at hub height. These systems are placed on the ground near the wind turbine and use laser technology to measure wind speeds and directions. Nacelle-mounted Lidar systems, on the other hand, are mounted on the wind turbine nacelle itself. These systems provide real-time data on wind conditions directly from the turbine, allowing for more accurate and efficient wind energy production. Both types of Lidar systems are essential for optimizing wind turbine performance and increasing energy production.</p></p>
