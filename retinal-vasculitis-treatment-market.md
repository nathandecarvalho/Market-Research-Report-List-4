<p><p>Retinal vasculitis treatment market includes two main types of drugs: immunosuppressive drugs and corticosteroids. Immunosuppressive drugs work by suppressing the body's immune response, reducing inflammation in the blood vessels of the retina. Corticosteroids, on the other hand, reduce inflammation by inhibiting the production of inflammatory chemicals. Both types of drugs are used to manage symptoms of retinal vasculitis and prevent further damage to the blood vessels in the eye. These drugs are commonly prescribed by ophthalmologists to patients with this condition.</p></p>
