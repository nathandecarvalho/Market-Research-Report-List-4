<p><p>Performance Polyolefins Market Types include LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), and others. LDPE is known for its flexibility and toughness, suitable for packaging. LLDPE offers higher tensile strength and puncture resistance, commonly used in stretch film. HDPE is renowned for its stiffness and strength, widely utilized in bottles and pipes. Other polyolefins include UHMWPE (Ultra-High Molecular Weight Polyethylene) and PEX (Cross-linked Polyethylene), which offer unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
