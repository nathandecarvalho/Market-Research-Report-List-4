<p><p>Urethral stricture market is divided into two main types: Anterior Urethral Stricture (AUS) and Posterior Urethral Stricture (PUS). AUS occurs in the front part of the urethra, while PUS occurs in the back part near the bladder. Both types can cause symptoms such as difficulty urinating, frequent urinary tract infections, and urinary retention. Treatment options for urethral strictures include dilation procedures, urethroplasty, and urethrotomy. Management is aimed at relieving symptoms and improving quality of life for patients.</p></p>
