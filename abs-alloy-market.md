<p><p>ABS Alloy market can be categorized into three main types: ABS/PC, ABS/PET, and Other ABS alloys. ABS/PC alloys are a blend of acrylonitrile-butadiene-styrene and polycarbonate, providing high impact resistance and heat resistance. ABS/PET alloys combine ABS with polyethylene terephthalate, offering good chemical resistance and dimensional stability. Other ABS alloys include blends with various additives and modifiers to achieve specific properties such as increased toughness or UV resistance. Each type caters to different industry needs and applications within the ABS Alloy market.</p></p>
