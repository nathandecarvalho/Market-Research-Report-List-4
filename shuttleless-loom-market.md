<p><strong>The Shuttleless Loom Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rapier (Single and Double Type)</li><li>Air Jet Loom</li><li>Water Jet Loom</li><li>Gripper or Projectile Loom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shuttleless looms have several market types based on their technology. Rapier looms are available in single and double types, with the ability to weave a wide range of fabrics efficiently. Air jet looms use compressed air for weft insertion, offering high speed and versatility. Water jet looms utilize water jets to insert the weft yarn, suitable for weaving lightweight fabrics. Gripper or projectile looms use grippers or projectiles to insert the weft yarn, providing precision and consistency in fabric production. Each type caters to specific needs and preferences within the textile industry.</p></p>
