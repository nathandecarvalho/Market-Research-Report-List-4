<p><ul><li>Full-Floating Axle Parts</li><li>Semi-Floating Axle Parts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive axle parts market can be segmented into two main types: full-floating axle parts and semi-floating axle parts. Full-floating axle parts are designed to support the weight of the vehicle directly on the axle shaft, allowing for higher weight capacities and smoother operation. Semi-floating axle parts, on the other hand, support the weight of the vehicle on the axle housing, with the axle shaft acting mainly as a driving component. These different types cater to varying needs and applications within the automotive industry.</p></p>
