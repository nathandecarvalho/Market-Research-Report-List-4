<p><p>THz detection and inspection market types include active Terahertz and passive Terahertz technologies. Active Terahertz systems use an external source to generate THz waves for detection and inspection purposes. Passive Terahertz systems, on the other hand, rely on naturally occurring THz radiation for detection and inspection. Both technologies have their own sets of advantages and applications in various industries such as security, healthcare, and manufacturing. Active Terahertz systems offer higher sensitivity and control, while passive Terahertz systems are typically less complex and require no external power source.</p></p>
