<p><strong>The Cast Acrylic Sheets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell Cast Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Continuous Cast Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cast acrylic sheets are available in two main types: Cell Cast Acrylic Sheet and Continuous Cast Acrylic Sheet. Cell cast acrylic sheets are produced by pouring liquid acrylic into molds and allowing it to dry, resulting in a high-quality, clear finish. Continuous cast acrylic sheets are made by continuously pouring liquid acrylic between two sheets of glass, resulting in a more cost-effective option with a lower optical quality. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications based on their specific properties.</p></p>
