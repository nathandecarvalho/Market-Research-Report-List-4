<p><p>Grounding busbar trunking systems are commonly available in two types: segregated bus duct and nonsegregated bus duct. Segregated bus duct systems have individual conductors separated by insulating barriers, providing added safety and protection against faults. On the other hand, nonsegregated bus duct systems have conductors grouped together without barriers, offering a more compact design and easier installation. Both types cater to different needs and specifications, making them suitable for various applications in industrial, commercial, and residential settings.</p></p>
