<p><p>Narrow Band IoT operates in three main market types: In-Band, Guard Band, and Standalone. In-Band utilizes existing cellular networks to transmit data, allowing for efficient use of resources. Guard Band operates in unused frequency spectrum, providing a dedicated channel for NB-IoT devices. Standalone operates independently, without relying on existing networks, offering complete control over communication. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences, providing flexibility and versatility for various IoT applications.</p></p>
