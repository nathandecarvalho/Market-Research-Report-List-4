<p><p>CNC 2D Laser Cutting Machines are available in two main types: CO2 2D Laser Machines and Fiber 2D Laser Machines. CO2 machines use a gas laser beam to cut materials like wood, plastic, or acrylic, while fiber machines utilize a fiber laser for more efficient cutting of metals. CO2 machines are commonly used for non-metal materials, while fiber machines are preferred for metal cutting due to their higher energy density. Both types cater to different market segments based on material requirements and cutting precision needs.</p></p>
