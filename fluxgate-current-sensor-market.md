<p><ul><li>Single Magnetic Ring</li><li>Double Magnetic Ring</li><li>Multi-magnetic Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluxgate current sensors are categorized by the number of magnetic rings they utilize. Single magnetic ring sensors employ one magnetic ring to measure current, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Double magnetic ring sensors use two rings for improved accuracy and reliability. Multi-magnetic ring sensors utilize multiple rings for even higher precision and sensitivity in current measurement. Each type provides varying levels of performance capabilities to cater to different application requirements in industries such as energy, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
