<p><p>Polymer Based Thermal Interface Materials (TIM) come in various forms including Polymer Based Thermal Sheets, Polymer Based Thermal Tapes, and Polymer Based Thermal Liquids. Polymer Based Thermal Sheets are solid thermal interface materials that are cut to the required size and shape. Polymer Based Thermal Tapes are adhesive thermal interface materials that can be easily attached to surfaces. Polymer Based Thermal Liquids are flowable thermal interface materials that can conform to complex shapes. Each type of TIM offers unique benefits and is used in different applications based on its properties and characteristics.</p></p>
