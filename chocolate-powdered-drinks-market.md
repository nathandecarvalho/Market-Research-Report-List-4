<p><p>Chocolate powdered drinks come in various types, including freeze dried, spray dried, and chicory based. Freeze dried products involve freezing the liquid chocolate before removing the water through sublimation. Spray dried powders are created by spraying liquid chocolate into hot air to dry it quickly. Chicory based drinks are made by blending chocolate with chicory root, offering a unique and slightly bitter flavor. Each type caters to different preferences and processing methods, offering varied options in the powdered drinks market.</p></p>
