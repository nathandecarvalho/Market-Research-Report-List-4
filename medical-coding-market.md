<p><p>Medical coding market types refer to the different systems used in healthcare settings to accurately code and classify medical procedures and diagnoses. The Healthcare Universal Program Coding System (HCPCS) is used primarily for identifying services, supplies, and equipment in healthcare settings. The International Classification of Diseases (ICD) is used for coding diagnoses, while the Current Procedural Terminology (CPT) system is used for coding medical procedures. These coding systems are essential for accurate billing, insurance claims, and research in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
