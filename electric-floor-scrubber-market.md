<p><p>The electric floor scrubber market consists of three main types: Walk-Behind Floor Scrubbers, Ride-On Floor Scrubbers, and Stand-On Floor Scrubbers. Walk-Behind Floor Scrubbers are manually operated and are ideal for smaller spaces. Ride-On Floor Scrubbers are larger machines that are driven like a vehicle, making them suitable for larger areas. Stand-On Floor Scrubbers offer a balance between maneuverability and productivity, allowing operators to stand while operating the machine. Each type caters to different cleaning needs and preferences in various commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
