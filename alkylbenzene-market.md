<p><p>Alkylbenzene market can be categorized into linear alkylbenzene and branched alkylbenzene. Linear alkylbenzene is a straight-chain hydrocarbon structure, while branched alkylbenzene has side chains branching off the main benzene ring. Linear alkylbenzene is widely used in household detergents, whereas branched alkylbenzene is preferred in industrial applications due to its higher thermal stability and resistance to degradation. Both types of alkylbenzene have specific uses and advantages in various industries, catering to different market segments.</p></p>
