<p><p>Laminating adhesives are used in the industrial market for bonding flexible materials together. Solvent-based laminating adhesives utilize a solvent to disperse the adhesive components, while solventless laminating adhesives rely on a chemical reaction to bond the materials. Solvent-based adhesives are known for their fast drying time and strong bond, while solventless adhesives are favored for being more environmentally friendly and having higher heat resistance. Both types offer unique benefits and are used in various industries for laminating applications.</p></p>
