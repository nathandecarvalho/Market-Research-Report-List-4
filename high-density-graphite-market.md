<p><p>High Density Graphite market can be classified into various types based on their manufacturing process. Isotropic Graphite is produced using high-quality aggregates and binder materials, resulting in uniform properties in all directions. Extruded Graphite is formed by extruding a mixture of graphite and binder through a die, creating a specific shape. Molded Graphite is made by compressing a mixture of graphite and binder into a mold. Other types of High Density Graphite include vibration molded graphite and die-molded graphite.</p></p>
