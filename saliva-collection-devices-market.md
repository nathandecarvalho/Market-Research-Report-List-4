<p><p>Saliva collection devices market can be broadly classified into two types: whole saliva collection devices and glandular saliva collection devices. Whole saliva collection devices are designed to collect saliva from the entire mouth, providing a comprehensive sample for analysis. Glandular saliva collection devices, on the other hand, are specifically designed to target and collect saliva from specific salivary glands, allowing for more targeted analysis. Both types of devices play a crucial role in various fields such as research, diagnostics, and personalized medicine.</p></p>
