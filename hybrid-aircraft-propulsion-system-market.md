<p><p>Hybrid aircraft propulsion systems have different types available in the market, including Parallel Hybrid Sugar Volt, Fully Turboelectric, and Partially Turboelectric systems. Parallel Hybrid Sugar Volt systems integrate electric motors and internal combustion engines to power the aircraft simultaneously. Fully Turboelectric systems utilize electric motors for propulsion without any combustion engines involved. Partially Turboelectric systems combine traditional engines with electric motors to provide power to the aircraft. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in the aviation industry.</p></p>
