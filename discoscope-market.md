<p><p>Discoscope market is divided into two types based on the number of channels: Multi-channel and Single channel. Multi-channel discoscopes are equipped with multiple channels to allow for simultaneous viewing from different angles. This type is commonly used in more complex medical procedures requiring a comprehensive view of the internal organs. Single channel discoscopes, on the other hand, have only one channel for viewing, making them suitable for simpler procedures where a single viewpoint is sufficient. Both types serve different purposes based on the complexity of the medical procedure.</p></p>
