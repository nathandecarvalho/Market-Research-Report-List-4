<p><p>Industrial laser printers are divided into different market types based on the laser technology used in the printing process. The main types include fiber laser, CO2 laser, UV laser, and others. Fiber laser printers use a fiber optic laser source for high precision printing. CO2 laser printers utilize a carbon dioxide gas laser for engraving and cutting applications. UV laser printers use ultraviolet light for marking on various materials. Other types of industrial laser printers may use different laser sources suited for specific printing needs.</p></p>
