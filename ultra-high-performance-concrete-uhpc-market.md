<p><p>Ultra High Performance Concrete (UHPC) is a type of concrete known for its exceptional strength, durability, and ductility. Three main types of UHPC on the market include Slurry-infiltrated Fibrous Concrete, Reactive Powder Concrete, and Compact Reinforced composite. Slurry-infiltrated Fibrous Concrete is made by infiltrating concrete with a slurry of cement and fibers, enhancing its toughness and ductility. Reactive Powder Concrete consists of ultrafine particles that react together to form a dense and strong material. Compact Reinforced Composite incorporates steel fibers to improve the concrete's properties and performance.</p></p>
