<p><ul><li>Solution Dry Spinning</li><li>Solution Wet Spinning</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spandex Fiber market is categorized into different types, such as Solution Dry Spinning, Solution Wet Spinning, and Others. Solution Dry Spinning involves dissolving the polymer in a solvent before spinning it into fibers, while Solution Wet Spinning involves the extrusion of the polymer solution through spinnerets into a coagulating bath. The category of Others includes alternative methods of producing Spandex fibers that may not fall under the Solution Dry or Wet Spinning processes. Each type has its own unique characteristics and applications in the textile industry.</p></p>
