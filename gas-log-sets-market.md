<p><ul><li>Vented Gas Log Set</li><li>Ventless Gas Log Set</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas log sets are classified into two main types: Vented Gas Log Sets and Ventless Gas Log Sets. Vented gas log sets are designed to mimic the appearance of a real wood-burning fireplace by allowing the combustion gases to escape through a chimney, while ventless gas log sets do not require a chimney and release heat directly into the room. Both types offer the convenience of a gas fireplace without the need for actual firewood, providing a safe and efficient alternative heating option for homes.</p></p>
