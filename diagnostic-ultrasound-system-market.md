<p><ul><li>Color Doppler Ultrasound Imaging Systems</li><li>Black & White Ultrasound Imaging Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diagnostic ultrasound systems are categorized into two main types: Color Doppler Ultrasound Imaging Systems and Black & White Ultrasound Imaging Systems. Color Doppler systems use variations in frequency to map blood flow and produce color images, which are useful in diagnosing conditions like heart diseases. On the other hand, Black & White systems use grayscale imaging to visualize organs, tissues, and abnormalities within the body. Both types of systems play a crucial role in medical imaging for diagnosing a wide range of conditions and diseases.</p></p>
