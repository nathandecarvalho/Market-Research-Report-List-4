<p><p>Dry honey is a versatile product that comes in two main market types: granulated honey and powdered honey. Granulated honey is made by drying liquid honey into small granules, making it easy to sprinkle on food or mix into beverages. Powdered honey, on the other hand, is created by dehydrating liquid honey into a fine powder, which can be used as a sweetener or flavoring agent in a variety of dishes. Both granulated and powdered honey offer the convenience of honey without the sticky mess.</p></p>
