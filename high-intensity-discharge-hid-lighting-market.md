<p><p>High Intensity Discharge (HID) Lighting is a type of lighting technology that produces light by passing an electric current through a gaseous or vaporized substance, such as mercury, sodium, or metal halide, within a sealed glass bulb. HID lighting is known for its high efficiency, long lifespan, and bright, powerful light output, making it a popular choice for outdoor and industrial lighting applications.</p><p>The High Intensity Discharge (HID) Lighting Market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, with a projected Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 12.5% during the forecast period.
