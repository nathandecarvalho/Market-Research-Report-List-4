<p><p>Nailed parquet floor market types include engineered and solid options. Engineered nailed parquet floors are made up of multiple layers of wood, with the top layer being a hardwood veneer. This type of flooring is more stable and resistant to moisture than solid wood. Both options offer a stylish and durable flooring solution for residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
