<p><strong>The Boat Electric Propulsion Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaftline Propulsion</li><li>Pod Propulsion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boat Electric Propulsion Systems market is categorized into Shaftline Propulsion and Pod Propulsion. Shaftline Propulsion involves a traditional propulsion system where the motor is connected to the propeller through a driveshaft. On the other hand, Pod Propulsion eliminates the need for a driveshaft as the motor and propeller are integrated into a pod that can rotate 360 degrees for enhanced maneuverability. Both systems offer efficient and eco-friendly propulsion options for boats and are gaining popularity in the marine industry.</p></p>
