<p><p>Optical Low Pass Filters (OLPF) are used in digital cameras to reduce the effect of aliasing and moiré patterns in images. There are three main types of OLPF markets: Single Layer Filter, Two Layer Filter, and Multilayer Filter. Single Layer Filters have a single layer of optical material, Two Layer Filters have two separate layers with different properties, and Multilayer Filters have multiple layers designed to enhance filtering effectiveness. Each type offers different levels of performance and complexity to meet specific image processing needs.</p></p>
