<p><p>Blister lidding foil is available in three main types. Single Layer (Push Through) foil is made of a single layer of material that can be easily pushed through when opening the blister pack. 2-Layer (Paper/Foil) foil consists of a paper layer for easy printing and a foil layer for protection. Child Resistant Foil is designed with special features to make it difficult for children to open the packaging. Each type serves specific needs and provides different benefits for packaging applications.</p></p>
