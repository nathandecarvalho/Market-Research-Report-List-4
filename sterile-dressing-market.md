<p><strong>The Sterile Dressing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transparent Sterile Dressing</li><li>Non-woven Sterile Dressing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transparent Sterile Dressings are clear adhesive coverings that allow visibility of the wound for monitoring without having to remove the dressing. These are commonly used for minor wounds, abrasions, and burns. On the other hand, Non-woven Sterile Dressings are made of soft, flexible material that provides cushioning and protection for wounds. They are often used for larger wounds, post-surgical sites, and to secure IV lines. Both types of sterile dressings help promote wound healing and prevent contamination.</p></p>
