<p><ul><li>Overhead Transmission</li><li>Underground Transmission</li><li>Subsea Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HVDC transmission systems are utilized in various applications including overhead transmission, underground transmission, and subsea transmission. Overhead transmission involves the use of high voltage cables suspended on towers, ideal for long-distance power transmission. Underground transmission involves the installation of cables underneath the ground, suitable for urban areas with limited space for overhead lines. Subsea transmission involves transferring electricity beneath bodies of water, commonly used for connecting offshore wind farms to the grid. These applications cater to the specific needs and challenges of different environments.</p></p>
