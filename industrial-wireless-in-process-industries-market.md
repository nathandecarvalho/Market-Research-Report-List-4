<p><p>Industrial wireless in process industries is utilized through different market types including Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN), Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN), Satellite (GNSS), Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN), and Cellular. WLAN provides high-speed wireless internet within a limited area, WPAN connects personal devices within a range of a few meters, GNSS uses satellite technology for location tracking, LPWAN enables long-range communication with low power consumption, and Cellular networks provide wireless voice and data communication through mobile devices. These market types cater to different industrial wireless needs in process industries.</p></p>
