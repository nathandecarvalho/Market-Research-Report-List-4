<p><strong>The Fiberglass Recycling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grinding</li><li>Incineration</li><li>Pyrolysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiberglass recycling can be done through various methods, including grinding, incineration, and pyrolysis. </p><p>In the grinding market, the fiberglass is mechanically shredded into smaller pieces for reuse or resale. In the incineration market, the fiberglass is burned at high temperatures to generate energy. </p><p>In the pyrolysis market, the fiberglass is broken down through a chemical process involving high heat in the absence of oxygen, which produces valuable byproducts.</p><p>Each method offers a different approach to recycling fiberglass waste and has its own benefits and limitations.</p></p>
