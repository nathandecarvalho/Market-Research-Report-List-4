<p><p>Hydrocarbon refrigerants are divided into three main types: propane, isobutane, and others. Propane and isobutane are the most commonly used hydrocarbons in refrigeration due to their low environmental impact and energy efficiency. They are popular choices for domestic refrigerators, air conditioning units, and commercial refrigeration systems. The "Others" category includes various hydrocarbons such as butane, pentane, and cyclopentane, which are also used as refrigerants in specific applications where their properties are beneficial.</p></p>
