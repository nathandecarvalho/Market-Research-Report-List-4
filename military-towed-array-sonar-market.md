<p><ul><li>Passive Sonar</li><li>Active Sonar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Military Towed Array Sonar market includes two main types: Passive Sonar and Active Sonar. Passive Sonar detects sound waves emitted by other vessels or marine life, providing stealth detection capabilities. Active Sonar emits pulses of sound waves and analyzes the echoes to detect objects underwater, offering a more precise method of target identification. Both systems play a crucial role in anti-submarine warfare operations, with Passive Sonar being more discreet and Active Sonar offering enhanced detection capabilities.</p></p>
