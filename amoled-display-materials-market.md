<p><p>AMOLED Display Materials Market Types: AMOLED Display refers to Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode technology, which offers higher resolution and faster refresh rates compared to Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) displays. AMOLED displays use a thin film transistor backplane to control each individual pixel, resulting in more vibrant colors and better image quality. PMOLED displays, on the other hand, are simpler and less expensive, but are limited in resolution and refresh rate. Both technologies have their own advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
