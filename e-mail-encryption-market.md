<p><p>E-mail encryption market types include SMTP STARTTLS, S/MIME, and PGP. SMTP STARTTLS enhances the security of email transmissions by encrypting the communication channel between mail servers. S/MIME (Secure/Multipurpose Internet Mail Extensions) provides end-to-end encryption for email messages, ensuring confidentiality and authentication. PGP (Pretty Good Privacy) is a widely-used encryption program that enables users to secure their emails with digital signatures and encryption. These technologies play a crucial role in safeguarding sensitive information in the digital landscape.</p></p>
