<p><ul><li>Power Sourcing Equipment</li><li>Powered Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Power Over Ethernet market can be categorized into two main types: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) and Powered Devices (PD). PSE refers to devices that supply power over Ethernet cables to connected PDs, such as cameras or sensors. PDs, on the other hand, are devices that receive power from PSE and use it to operate without the need for a separate power source. Both types play a crucial role in enabling the deployment of networked devices in industrial settings.</p></p>
