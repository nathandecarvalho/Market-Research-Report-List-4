<p><p>Waste Heat Recovery Boilers are used to recover heat from exhaust gases in industrial processes. There are three main types of Waste Heat Recovery Boilers: Water Tube Boilers, Fire Tube Boilers, and Others. Water Tube Boilers use water-filled tubes to transfer heat, while Fire Tube Boilers have hot gases passing through tubes surrounded by water. Other types of Waste Heat Recovery Boilers include exhaust gas boilers and heat exchangers. Each type has its own advantages and suitability for different industrial applications.</p></p>
