<p><p>High blood pressure drugs market can be categorized into ACE inhibitors (ACEI), calcium channel blockers (CCB), and angiotensin II receptor blockers (ARB). ACE inhibitors work by blocking the conversion of angiotensin I to angiotensin II, leading to vasodilation and decreased blood pressure. Calcium channel blockers inhibit the influx of calcium into cardiac and smooth muscle cells, relaxing blood vessels and reducing blood pressure. ARBs block the action of angiotensin II, resulting in vasodilation and decreased blood pressure. Each type of medication plays a crucial role in managing hypertension effectively.</p></p>
