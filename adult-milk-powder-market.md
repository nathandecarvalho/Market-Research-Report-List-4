<p><strong>The Adult Milk Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole Milk Powder</li><li>Skim Milk Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adult milk powder market can be broadly classified into two main types: Whole Milk Powder and Skim Milk Powder. Whole Milk Powder is made by evaporating the water content from fresh milk, resulting in a powder that contains all the natural fats and nutrients found in milk. Skim Milk Powder, on the other hand, is produced by removing most of the fat content from fresh milk before evaporating the water, resulting in a powder with lower fat content. Both types are popular choices for consumers looking for convenient and long-lasting dairy products.</p></p>
