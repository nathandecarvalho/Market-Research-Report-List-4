<p><p>Floating offshore wind turbines can be categorized into two main types: horizontal axis wind turbines (HAWT) and vertical axis wind turbines (VAWT). HAWTs have blades rotating around a horizontal axis and are considered more common in the market. On the other hand, VAWTs have blades spinning around a vertical axis, offering benefits such as lower noise levels and easier maintenance. Both types play a crucial role in the rapidly growing floating offshore wind turbine market by harnessing wind energy to generate electricity.</p></p>
