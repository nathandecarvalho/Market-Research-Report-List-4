<p><p>The connected bulb market is divided into three main types: ZigBee, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth. ZigBee bulbs form a mesh network for reliable communication, making them ideal for smart home setups. Wi-Fi bulbs connect directly to a home network, offering easy installation and control via mobile apps. Bluetooth bulbs are simple to set up and operate, but have a shorter range compared to ZigBee and Wi-Fi options. Each type offers unique features and benefits to cater to different consumer needs.</p></p>
