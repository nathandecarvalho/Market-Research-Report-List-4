<p><p>Dispenser controller market can be classified into two types - Compensation Type and Non-compensated Type. In Compensation Type market, the controller adjusts the amount dispensed based on varying conditions such as temperature, pressure, or viscosity to ensure accurate dispensing. On the other hand, in Non-compensated Type market, the controller dispenses a fixed amount without adjusting for external factors. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of customers depending on the level of precision and control required in dispensing applications.</p></p>
