<p><p>Metal packaging materials market includes two main types - aluminium packaging and steel packaging. Aluminium packaging is lightweight, recyclable, and offers protection from light, moisture, and gases. It is commonly used for packaging beverages, food, pharmaceuticals, and personal care products. On the other hand, steel packaging is durable, tamper-resistant, and provides excellent barrier properties against moisture and gases. It is widely used for packaging food, paint, chemicals, and industrial products. Both aluminium and steel packaging are sustainable options for preserving and protecting goods.</p></p>
