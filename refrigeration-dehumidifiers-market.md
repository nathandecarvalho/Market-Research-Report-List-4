<p><p>Refrigeration dehumidifiers are classified into three main types: Normal Type, Heat Pipe Type, and New Air Type. </p><p>Normal Type dehumidifiers operate by passing air over cold coils to condense and remove moisture. Heat Pipe Type dehumidifiers use a heat exchange process to remove moisture from the air. New Air Type dehumidifiers work by combining the functions of air conditioning and dehumidifying to provide a more efficient and effective solution for controlling humidity levels. Each type offers unique benefits and features to suit different needs and preferences.</p></p>
