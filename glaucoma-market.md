<p><p>Glaucoma is a serious eye condition that requires medical intervention to prevent vision loss. There are two main types of treatments available in the market: Conventional Glaucoma Surgeries and Minimally Invasive Glaucoma Surgeries. Conventional surgeries involve traditional methods like trabeculectomy or tube shunt implantation, while Minimally Invasive Surgeries utilize newer techniques such as microstent implants or laser procedures. Both types aim to lower intraocular pressure and prevent further damage to the optic nerve, ultimately preserving the patient's eyesight.</p></p>
