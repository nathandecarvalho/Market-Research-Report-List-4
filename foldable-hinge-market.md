<p><ul><li>Single Shaft</li><li>Multiple Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foldable hinge market is divided into two main types: single shaft and multiple shaft hinges. Single shaft hinges consist of one central pivot point, allowing for a simple folding mechanism. Multiple shaft hinges, on the other hand, have two or more pivot points, providing added flexibility and range of motion. While single shaft hinges are commonly used in basic folding applications, multiple shaft hinges are preferred for more complex folding designs that require greater adjustability and durability.</p></p>
