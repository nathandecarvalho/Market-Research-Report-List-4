<p><p>Band pass electronic filter market consists of passive electronic filters and active electronic filters. Passive electronic filters do not require an external power source and are composed of passive components such as resistors, capacitors, and inductors. Active electronic filters, on the other hand, require an external power source and utilize active components such as transistors or operational amplifiers to achieve signal processing. Both types of filters play a crucial role in controlling the frequency response of electronic circuits.</p></p>
