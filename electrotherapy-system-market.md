<p><p>Electrotherapy systems market includes various types such as Extracorporeal Shock Wave Therapy, Magnetic Field Therapy, Ultrasound Therapy, Microcurrent Therapy, Interferential Current Therapy, and others. Extracorporeal Shock Wave Therapy uses high-energy shock waves to treat musculoskeletal conditions. Magnetic Field Therapy involves the use of electromagnetic fields for pain relief. Ultrasound Therapy uses high-frequency sound waves to promote healing. Microcurrent Therapy delivers low electrical currents to stimulate tissue repair. Interferential Current Therapy uses different frequencies of electrical currents for pain relief. Other types include TENS and EMS therapy.</p></p>
