<p><ul><li>Single Axis</li><li>Dual Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Solar Tracker market includes two main types - Single Axis and Dual Axis trackers. Single Axis trackers move in one direction to follow the sun's path throughout the day, typically from east to west. Dual Axis trackers move in two directions, both horizontally and vertically, to optimize solar panel orientation and increase energy production. While Single Axis trackers are more common and cost-effective, Dual Axis trackers offer higher efficiency and energy output but come with a higher price tag.</p></p>
