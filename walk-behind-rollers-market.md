<p><strong>The Walk-Behind Rollers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Drum Rollers</li><li>Double Drum Rollers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Walk-behind rollers are commonly classified into two main types - Single-Drum Rollers and Double Drum Rollers. Single-Drum Rollers have a large drum at the front of the machine, which provides traction and compaction force. On the other hand, Double Drum Rollers have two drums, one at the front and one at the back, that work together to provide smooth and even compaction. These rollers are mainly used for compacting soil, gravel, asphalt, and other materials in construction and landscaping projects.</p></p>
