<p><strong>The Pickup Truck Disc Brake Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Opposed Piston</li><li>Floating Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pickup truck disc brake market includes two main types: opposed piston and floating caliper. Opposed piston brakes have pistons on both sides of the disc, providing more evenly distributed pressure for better braking performance. Floating caliper brakes have a single piston that applies pressure on one side of the disc. Opposed piston brakes are typically seen in high-performance vehicles for their superior braking power, while floating calipers are more commonly found in everyday vehicles for their simplicity and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
