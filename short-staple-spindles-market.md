<p><ul><li>Supported Spindles</li><li>Bottom Whorl Drop Spindles</li><li>Top Whorl Drop Spindles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Short-Staple Spindles Market includes three main types - Supported Spindles, Bottom Whorl Drop Spindles, and Top Whorl Drop Spindles. Supported spindles are fixed in a holder while spinning, providing stability. Bottom whorl drop spindles have a whorl at the bottom, creating a longer spin time. Top whorl drop spindles have the whorl at the top, allowing for faster spinning. Each type caters to different spinning preferences and techniques, making them popular choices among textile artisans and hobbyists.</p></p>
