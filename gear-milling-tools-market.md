<p><p>The gear milling tools market includes various types such as gear hobs, milling cutters, rack milling tools, gear shaper cutters, shaving tools, master gears, ring & plug gauges, and broaches. Gear hobs are used for the cutting of gears, while milling cutters are used for gear profiling. Rack milling tools are used for cutting gear racks, while gear shaper cutters are used for shaping gears. Shaving tools are used for finishing gears, while master gears are used for calibration. Ring & plug gauges are used for measuring gear dimensions, and broaches are used for cutting gear teeth.</p></p>
