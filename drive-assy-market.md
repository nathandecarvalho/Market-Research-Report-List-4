<p><p>Drive assembly market types include Chain and Sprocket, Gear and Pinion, Converters, Motors, Coupling, and Others. Chain and Sprocket systems use a chain to transmit power between two sprockets. Gear and Pinion systems utilize gears to transfer power. Converters change the speed or torque of the power transmission. Motors provide the energy to drive the assembly. Couplings connect two shafts together to transmit power. Other types of drive assemblies may include belt drives, pulley drives, and hydraulic drives.</p></p>
