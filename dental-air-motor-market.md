<p><ul><li>Internal Spray</li><li>External Spray</li><li>Without Spray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental air motors come in three main types based on the spray mechanism they use - Internal Spray, External Spray, and Without Spray. Internal Spray air motors have a built-in water spray system for cooling and irrigation during procedures. External Spray air motors have a separate water spray attachment for the same purpose. Without Spray air motors do not have a water spray function, making them suitable for dry procedures. Dentists can choose the type of air motor that best suits their needs and preferences.</p></p>
