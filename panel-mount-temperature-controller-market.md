<p><p>Panel-mount temperature controllers are devices used to control temperature in various industrial applications. There are three main types of temperature controllers: Switch Controller, Proportional Controller, and PID Controller.</p><p>Switch Controllers are simple on/off devices that activate heating or cooling systems based on preset temperature thresholds.</p><p>Proportional Controllers adjust the output based on the difference between the desired and actual temperatures, providing more precise control than Switch Controllers.</p><p>PID Controllers use a combination of proportional, integral, and derivative control to quickly and accurately stabilize temperature fluctuations.</p></p>
