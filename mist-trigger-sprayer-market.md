<p><p>Mist trigger sprayers come in two main types: PP and PE. PP (polypropylene) trigger sprayers are known for their durability, chemical resistance, and ability to withstand high temperatures. They are commonly used in household cleaning products, personal care items, and agricultural chemicals. On the other hand, PE (polyethylene) trigger sprayers are more flexible and have good impact resistance, making them suitable for a variety of applications such as gardening, automotive, and industrial cleaning products. Both PP and PE trigger sprayers offer reliable performance and are widely available in the market.</p></p>
