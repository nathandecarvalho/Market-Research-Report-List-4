<p><p>Banana flour is available in both organic and conventional markets. Organic banana flour is produced without the use of synthetic chemicals or pesticides, making it a healthier option for those concerned about exposure to harmful substances. It is often more expensive due to the additional cost involved in organic farming practices. Conventional banana flour, on the other hand, is produced using traditional methods and may contain residues of chemicals used in farming. It is generally more affordable and widely available in mainstream grocery stores. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and price points.</p></p>
