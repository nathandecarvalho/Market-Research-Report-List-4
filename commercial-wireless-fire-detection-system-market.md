<p><p>The Commercial Wireless Fire Detection System Market includes two main types: Addressable Wireless Fire Detection System and Conventional Wireless Fire Detection System. Addressable systems allow for each detector to have a unique address, making it easier to pinpoint the exact location of a fire. Conventional systems, on the other hand, divide a building into zones and indicate which zone may have a fire. Both types offer wireless capabilities, providing flexibility and ease of installation in commercial buildings.</p></p>
