<p><p>Flat belt conveyors are commonly used in industries for transporting various materials. There are two main types of flat belt conveyors: open belt conveyors and enclosed belt conveyors. Open belt conveyors have no covering over the belt, allowing for easy access to the material being transported. Enclosed belt conveyors, on the other hand, have a protective covering over the belt, providing a cleaner and more secure transport environment. Both types serve different purposes based on the specific needs of the industry utilizing them.</p></p>
