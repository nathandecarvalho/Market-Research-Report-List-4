<p><strong>The Front Surface Mirror Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glass Substrates</li><li>Metallic Substrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Front surface mirrors are categorized based on the type of substrates used in their construction. Glass substrate mirrors are made by coating the front surface of a glass pane with a reflective material. These mirrors offer high optical clarity and are commonly used in scientific and industrial applications. Metallic substrate mirrors are constructed by depositing a reflective coating directly onto a metal surface. These mirrors are more durable and can withstand harsh environmental conditions, making them suitable for outdoor use in automotive and architectural applications.</p></p>
