<p><strong>The Railway Vehicle Toilet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Toilet System</li><li>Pressured Water Flushing Toilet System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Railway vehicle toilets are available in two main types: Vacuum Toilet System and Pressured Water Flushing Toilet System. </p><p>Vacuum Toilet System uses negative pressure to suck waste into a sealed container, reducing water usage and minimizing odors. It is ideal for trains where water availability is limited. </p><p>Pressured Water Flushing Toilet System, on the other hand, uses water under pressure to flush waste into a holding tank. This system is more traditional and requires more water but ensures a thorough flush and clean environment.</p></p>
