<p><p>Printer ink market is divided into two main types - Dye Type and Pigment Type. Dye ink is made of colorants dissolved in liquid, while pigment ink contains tiny solid particles that are suspended in a liquid base. Dye ink is known for producing vibrant colors and smooth gradients, ideal for photos and graphics. On the other hand, pigment ink is more resistant to fading and water damage, making it suitable for text documents and durable prints. Both types cater to different printing needs and preferences.</p></p>
