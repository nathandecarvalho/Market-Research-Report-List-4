<p><p>Automatic Keratometers are devices used to measure the curvature of the cornea for diagnosing various eye conditions. There are two main types of Automatic Keratometers on the market: Fixed Double Image Method and Variable Double Image Method. </p><p>Fixed Double Image Method keratometers have a fixed distance between the two images used for measurement, while Variable Double Image Method keratometers allow for adjustment of the image separation distance. Both types are used in ophthalmology practices for accurate corneal measurements and diagnosis of conditions such as astigmatism.</p></p>
