<p><strong>The Camshaft Assy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Overhead Camshaft(OHC)</li><li>One-piece Camshaft(OPC)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Camshaft Assembly market can be divided into three main types: Overhead Camshaft (OHC), One-piece Camshaft (OPC), and Others. OHC camshafts are located above the cylinder head and are driven by a belt or chain, offering better performance and fuel efficiency. OPC camshafts are a single piece that combines both intake and exhaust lobes, reducing complexity and cost. The Others category includes various camshaft designs that do not fall under OHC or OPC types, catering to specific engine configurations and performance requirements.</p></p>
