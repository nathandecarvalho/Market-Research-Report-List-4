<p><p>Infusion extension lines are categorized into different types based on the number of channels they have for medication delivery. One-way extension lines allow for the administration of a single medication at a time. Two-way extension lines allow for the administration of two medications simultaneously. Three-way extension lines have three channels for multiple medication administration. Four-way extension lines have four channels for delivering multiple medications or fluids concurrently. Each type of extension line serves a specific purpose in medical settings for efficient and accurate medication delivery.</p></p>
