Floor-standing analyzers are larger in size and are designed for high-volume testing in large laboratories. They offer higher throughput and can accommodate a larger number of samples compared to bench-top analyzers. Bench-top analyzers, on the other hand, are smaller and more compact, making them suitable for smaller laboratories or point-of-care testing. They are more portable and space-efficient than their floor-standing counterparts.</p></p>
